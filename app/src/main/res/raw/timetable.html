Please note that there will be some adjustments to the timetable as students change modules or degree schemes during the first few weeks of term. If this is the case; don’t worry; but do avoid making firm plans (i.e. for part time work) based on the initial version of the timetable. The timetable will settle after the first few weeks of term.
